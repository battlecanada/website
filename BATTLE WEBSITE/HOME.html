ABOUT US

Battle Canada is a national organization dedicated to growing basketball and uniting communities across the country. More than just courts and tournaments, we’re building a culture that celebrates the love of the game at every level—from grassroots players learning the fundamentals to elite athletes pushing the boundaries of competition. Our state-of-the-art facilities, dynamic events, and inclusive programs are designed to bring people together, inspire excellence, and create opportunities for growth both on and off the court. At Battle Canada, basketball isn’t just a game; it’s a way to connect, empower, and leave a lasting impact in every community we serve.
